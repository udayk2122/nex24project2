Shipping & Returns Policy

We strive to provide the best service for all your plant needs. Here are the details of our policies.

Shipping Policy

  • All orders are processed and shipped within 3-5 business days.
  • We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹5000.
  • A flat rate of ₹200 applies to all orders below ₹5000.
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.
  • We currently only ship within India.

Returns Policy

  • We accept returns within 15 days of delivery.
  • Plants must be in their original condition and packaging.
  • A full refund will be issued for the cost of the plant, minus shipping fees.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@terra.com with your order number.
  • Returns are not accepted for plants damaged due to improper care.
